Implementation Considerations

Key factors for adding multilingual support to nashuacms.org

Recommended Languages

  • Spanish - Largest non-English community in Nashua
  • Portuguese - Significant Brazilian population
  • Vietnamese - Growing community presence
  • Chinese (Simplified) - Family demand
  • French - Quebec proximity, Haitian community

Squarespace Options

  • Weglot plugin - Auto-translation + manual editing
  • WPML alternative - Professional translation workflow
  • Google Translate widget - Free but lower quality
  • Manual duplicate pages - Full control, high effort

Priority Content

  • Scholarship application page
  • Program descriptions and enrollment
  • Contact and location information
  • Summer camp registration
  • Mission and "About Us" pages

Recommendation

Based on NCMS's goals and Squarespace constraints

Option A: Globe Dropdown + Weglot

The globe dropdown pattern is universally understood, works on mobile, and integrates seamlessly with Squarespace via Weglot. Start with Spanish and Portuguese translations for highest-impact communities, then expand based on demand.

Low implementation effort ~$15/month Auto + manual translation
