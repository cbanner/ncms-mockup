Design Rationale

1
Mission-Led Hero
The mission statement moves from a buried sub-page to the hero headline. Visitors immediately understand NCMS's purpose and values.
Boost Storytelling
2
40-Year Legacy Badge
A subtle but powerful credibility signal. Longevity builds trust for both donors and prospective families.
Boost Storytelling
3
Dual Call-to-Action
Current site only shows "Donate Today." Adding "Start Your Journey" creates a pathway for enrollment—serving both fundraising AND growth goals.
Build Infrastructure
4
Impact Statistics Bar
Concrete numbers (students, scholarships, programs, years) provide social proof and demonstrate community reach—key for donor confidence.
Boost Storytelling
5
Testimonial Section
A real parent voice creates emotional connection. Mentioning scholarships reinforces impact and encourages donations.
Bridge Communities
6
Dual CTA Panels
Clear, equal-weight pathways for donors AND prospective students. Neither audience has to hunt for their next step.
Build Infrastructure
